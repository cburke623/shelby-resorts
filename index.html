Bluebeard's Beach Club

  • Description
  • Resort Amenities
  • Helpful Hints
  • Area Activities
7511 Frenchman's Bay
St. Thomas , VI 00802
340-776-4770

Outstanding private, pristine beachside Wyndham resort in beautiful Frenchman's Bay, St. Thomas, VI. Enjoy private beach access and an outdoor free-form pool with a swim up bar. Be active with water sports such as snorkeling, scuba diving or kayaking, or use the on-site fitness center and tennis at the resort. The condos have a private balcony with a gorgeous ocean and pool view. Enjoy a “home” cooked meal in your condo or visit outstanding restaurants in the surrounding area. Charlotte Amalie is just 15 minutes away with great shopping and boutiques. Enjoy yourself in a tropical oasis!!
